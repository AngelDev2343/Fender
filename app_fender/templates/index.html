Who We Are?

Founded by Leo Fender in 1946, Fender Musical Instruments Corporation has revolutionized music worldwide. We are more than a guitar manufacturer; we are a global cultural icon that has powered artists and musicians for decades.

From the Telecaster® to the Stratocaster®, our creations have been the choice of music legends across all genres. Our commitment is to innovation, quality, and the spirit of rock 'n' roll, equipping musicians from beginners to professionals with the tools they need to express themselves.

Our influence doesn't stop at guitars. Leo Fender also introduced the Precision Bass® in 1951, the first commercially successful electric bass, which fundamentally changed the rhythm of popular music. Alongside our legendary amplifiers, such as the Twin Reverb® and the Bassman®, we have defined the sound of stages and studios for generations.

Today, the Fender legacy continues. We are a global community of musicians, artists, builders, and dreamers. We invite you to explore our collection, find your unique sound, and join the Fender family to write the next chapter in music history.
